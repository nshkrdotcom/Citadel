    Citadel.InvocationRequest (citadel v0.1.0)

    Copy Markdown

    Frozen Citadel-owned invoke seam handed to invocation_bridge.

    Wave 2 freezes carrier shape and ownership here. Wave 3 may tighten value mappings feeding boundary_intent and topology_intent, but incompatible carrier changes require an explicit schema_version step.
